Akan Naming Site

Welcome to Akan Naming Site.

Akan is a site that requests it users to input their gender and their date of birth.In return,
it provides the user with their Akan names according to the day day of the week they were born.

Here is the list of how the users will be given their names.

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
